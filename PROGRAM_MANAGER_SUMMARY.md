## 📋 3. Simple Weekly Action Guidelines for Field Staff

When supervisors run weekly audits, they must follow these three simple rules:

1. **Check the Attendance Log first**: If a child's attendance is below 80%, they will not transition levels. Talk to parents immediately.
2. **Flag Fast Jumps**: If a child jumps $> 2$ levels in one week (e.g., from Letter directly to Sentence/Division), mark them for a spot-test. Our M&E officer must confirm the score to keep data honest.
3. **Log Data in 24 Hours**: Ensure all Weekly Assessment sheets are filled out on Google Sheets by Saturday evening. This qualifies the field worker for the **₹200 monthly recharge card incentive** and prevents data submission lag.
