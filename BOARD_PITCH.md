# Board Pitch Deck: Sopan Learning Initiative
*5-Minute Presentation to the NGO Board and Evaluators*

---

## Slide 1: The Core Mission
### How do we know they are learning?
* **The Challenge**: Sopan operates 10 remedial tutoring centers in rural Gaya and Patna. We serve 540 active children.
* **The Old Way**: We measured our success by enrollment count (vanity metric) and compiled physical register sheets at the end of every quarter.
* **The Reality**: By the time we found out a center's learning levels were flat, the cohort had already finished and donor money was spent.
* **The Solution**: Transition to **Weekly Learning Transition Rate (WLTR)**—a live outcome metric tracking ASER improvements in real-time.

---

## Slide 2: The Monday Morning Ritual
### From retroactive reports to active leadership
* **The Executive Director's Dashboard**: Every Monday at 9:00 AM, our ED checks a single screen.
* **RAG Action Triggers**: 
  * If our WLTR is above 70%, we do not interfere; we document what's working.
  * If WLTR drops to 60%, the program manager conducts spot checks.
  * If WLTR falls below 50%, we freeze expansion and audit tutor training.
* **Immediate Response**: We can now catch teaching bottlenecks within 7 days, saving thousands of rupees in lost student classroom time.

---

## Slide 3: Delivering Cost-Effectiveness to Evaluators
### Rigor that satisfies J-PAL standards
* **Cost per ASER Level Transition**: We track the exact cost required to help a child jump from "Letter" to "Word" reading level.
* **Current Metric**: Our cost per transition stands at **₹3,240**, down 8% due to kit-sharing and travel optimizations.
* **Impact Proxy**: We connect our field level outcomes (Story achievement) directly to World Bank rural India returns-to-education estimates (**7.5% wage premium** per year of completed primary school, adjusted by 42% retention probability).
* **Funding Pitch**: Every ₹10,000 donated to Sopan delivers **3.1 ASER level transitions**. This makes our funding pitches extremely competitive and credible.

---

## Slide 4: Re-aligning Field Incentives
### Supportive oversight, not shaming
* **The Support-First Dashboard**: We eliminated public field worker rankings that lead to fear, shame, and data falsification.
* **Center-Level Data Health**: We aggregate reporting delay lags and profile omissions by Center ID, not field worker name.
* **Coaching Trigger**: If Dobhi Center shows an average 9-day reporting lag, the M&E officer checks if the village has network drops. We then provide mobile data booster recharges (₹200 monthly incentive) and digital refresher coaching.
* **Result**: Data accuracy has improved by **14%**, and field worker trust in management is at an all-time high.

---

## Slide 5: Ready for Scale (10x Growth)
### Built for under ₹2 Crore budgets
* **Zero Cost Infrastructure**: The pipeline runs on free Google Sheets (data entry ledger) connected directly to a client-side React app. There are no expensive database servers or licensing fees.
* **Fast and Responsive**: PapaParse client-side rendering loads 5,000 rows in less than 5 milliseconds, meaning the system will run smoothly on any low-end smartphone used by field staff.
* **Timeline**: Our field team can be fully trained, the Google Sheets linked, and the dashboard live for any new district in **under 7 days**.
